Owl

All owls have upright posture and forward-facing eyes that give them binocular vision, just like humans.

  • Latin order name: Strigiformes
  • Size range: 10cm - 60cm
  • Lifestyle: Nocturnal
  • Speciality: Silent flight

Unlike other raptors, owls are mostly nocturnal, hunting at night when the other raptors are sleeping.
