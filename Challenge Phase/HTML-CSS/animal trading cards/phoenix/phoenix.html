Phoenix

Their scarlet body feathers glow faintly in darkness, while their golden tail feathers are hot to the touch.

  • Feather color : Scarlet, golden tail
  • Native range : Egypt, India, China
  • Ministry of Magic Classification : XXXX
  • Speciality : Rebirthing mortal

The most startling of the phoenix's abilities is its ability to regenerate itself. It periodically bursts into flames when its body becomes old, and rises from the ashes as a newborn chick.
