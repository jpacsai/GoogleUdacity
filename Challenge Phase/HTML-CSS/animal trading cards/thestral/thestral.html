Thestral

They can only be seen by people who've seen death.

  • Hair colour: Black
  • Native range: British Isles, France, Iberian Peninsula, Ireland
  • Ministry of Magic Classification: XXXX
  • Size: Larger than a wingless horse

A Thestral is a breed of winged horses with a skeletal body, face with reptilian features, and wide, leathery wings that resemble a bat's.
